ABOUT ME

I’m Yixuan Cai, a 21 years old undergraduate student of Queens College, currently majoring in BFA Design.

“Elegant” has always been the word for my standard of design which is to maximize effect with minimum means.

I enjoy making beautiful designs due to my high passion. I also love drawing as well as painting. Hopefully, my work reflects that.
