The Dancing Geisha

ARTS (150-02) Fundamentals Of Art. 5 different textures were used in this artwork. 1. Real: yarn for her hair and silk for the smoke. 2. Rubbed: rubbing technique was used on the fan. 3. Drawn: pen and watercolor drawing. 4. manipulated: Cutting and tearing the silky scarf. 5. Reproduced: pattern paper for her clothing.
