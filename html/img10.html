VEZZINI&CHEN Art Gallery Brochure

This is an exhibition brochure designed specifically for contemporary sculpture artists Vezzini and Chen. This booklet contains information about Vezzini&Chens’ most famous works and their descriptions. All the contents are from Vezzini&Chen’s website. I did some research on their sculptures in order to fully understand and came out with a perfect color palette for it. I spent three weeks in class for this project, several revisions were made before the final version.
